# Safety Policy

## Unsaid

**Version:** v1.0
**Last Updated:** [Insert Date]
**Region:** India
**Language:** English

---

## 1. Purpose of This Policy

The Safety Policy defines how **Unsaid** protects users from emotional harm, exploitation, coercion, and unsafe interactions while maintaining anonymity and dignity.

Safety is a **core product requirement**, not a secondary feature. All platform design, moderation, and enforcement decisions prioritize user wellbeing over engagement or growth.

---

## 2. Safety Philosophy

Unsaid is built on the following principles:

1. **Prevention over reaction**
2. **Boundaries over emotional dependency**
3. **Support without substitution**
4. **Anonymity without irresponsibility**

Unsaid does not aim to manage crises or replace professional care. Its role is to provide a **safe space for expression and peer support**, with clear limits.

---

## 3. Scope of Safety Coverage

This policy applies to:

* All users
* All community spaces
* All peer conversations
* All professional-led sessions
* All moderation and enforcement actions

---

## 4. Prohibited Content and Behavior

### 4.1 Self-Harm and Suicide

**Strictly prohibited:**

* Encouraging self-harm or suicide
* Instructions, methods, or detailed descriptions
* Romanticizing death or harm

**Allowed with limits:**

* Expressions of distress, pain, or hopelessness
* Non-graphic emotional sharing

When risk indicators are detected, escalation procedures apply.

---

### 4.2 Graphic or Explicit Trauma Content

To protect community safety:

* No graphic descriptions of abuse, violence, or trauma
* No explicit reenactments or sensory details

Users are encouraged to focus on **emotions and impact**, not explicit events.

---

### 4.3 Emotional Manipulation and Dependency

The following behaviors are prohibited:

* “Only I understand you” or exclusivity language
* Pressure to continue conversations
* Guilt-based emotional reliance
* Discouraging off-platform support

---

### 4.4 Harassment, Abuse, and Discrimination

Unsaid has zero tolerance for:

* Harassment, threats, or intimidation
* Hate speech or discriminatory language
* Shaming, blaming, or dismissive behavior

---

### 4.5 Romantic, Sexual, or Grooming Behavior

Unsaid is **not a dating or relationship platform**.

Prohibited:

* Sexual or romantic advances
* Flirtation or suggestive language
* Requests to move conversations off-platform
* Grooming or coercive behavior

---

### 4.6 Privacy Violations

Users must not:

* Request or share personal identifying information
* Impersonate professionals or other users
* Share third-party private information

---

## 5. Anonymity and Safety Balance

While Unsaid protects anonymity:

* Abuse of anonymity is not tolerated
* Safety enforcement may override anonymity to restrict access
* All moderation decisions are content- and behavior-based

Unsaid does not collect real-world identity for enforcement.

---

## 6. Moderation Framework

### 6.1 Moderation Methods

Unsaid uses a layered approach:

* Automated detection systems
* Human moderation review
* Community reporting mechanisms

---

### 6.2 Enforcement Actions

Depending on severity and pattern:

* Content removal
* Temporary restrictions
* Session termination
* Permanent account suspension

Decisions consider intent, impact, and user safety history.

---

## 7. Crisis Escalation Protocol

### 7.1 Detection

High-risk signals include:

* Expressions of imminent self-harm intent
* Loss of protective factors
* Explicit statements of danger

---

### 7.2 Response

When detected:

* Conversations may be interrupted
* India-specific crisis resources are displayed
* Users are encouraged to seek off-platform help

**Unsaid does not intervene, track, or contact emergency services.**

---

## 8. Professional Safety Standards

* Only verified professionals may offer professional services
* All professional interactions are time-bound and structured
* No private, ongoing 1:1 therapeutic relationships are permitted
* Professionals must adhere to ethical and regulatory guidelines

Violations result in immediate suspension.

---

## 9. Reporting and Review

Users may report:

* Safety violations
* Boundary breaches
* Manipulative or harmful behavior

Reports are:

* Confidential
* Reviewed promptly
* Protected from retaliation

---

## 10. Continuous Safety Improvement

Unsaid commits to:

* Regular safety audits
* Policy updates based on emerging risks
* Consultation with mental health and safety experts

Safety policies may evolve without notice to address new concerns.

---

## 11. Legal and Regulatory Alignment (India)

This policy aligns with:

* Information Technology Act, 2000
* Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
* Applicable content moderation obligations

---

## 12. Final Statement

Unsaid believes that **being heard should never come at the cost of safety**.

This platform exists to support expression—not to replace real-world care, relationships, or responsibility.
