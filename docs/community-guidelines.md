## 1. Purpose of Unsaid

Unsaid exists to provide a **safe, anonymous, and judgment-free space** for individuals to share thoughts and feelings they may not feel comfortable expressing elsewhere.

Our community is built on:

* Respect
* Emotional safety
* Boundaries
* Non-judgment

These guidelines exist to protect users from harm, exploitation, coercion, and emotional dependency.

---

## 2. What Unsaid Is — and Is Not

### Unsaid **IS**:

* An anonymous peer-support platform
* A moderated space for sharing and listening
* A place for structured, time-bound conversations
* A bridge to professional mental health support

### Unsaid **IS NOT**:

* A crisis or emergency service
* A substitute for therapy or medical care
* A platform for graphic trauma disclosure
* A dating, romantic, or relationship-building app

---

## 3. Core Community Rules

All users must adhere to the following rules at all times.

### 3.1 Respect and Non-Judgment

* No shaming, blaming, mocking, or dismissive language
* No moral judgments or unsolicited criticism
* Validate feelings without labeling or diagnosing

**Allowed:**

> “That sounds really difficult. Thank you for sharing.”

**Not Allowed:**

> “You’re overreacting.”
> “This is your fault.”

---

### 3.2 Consent-Based Interaction

* Do not give advice unless the other user explicitly asks
* Do not pressure anyone to share more than they want
* Respect stated boundaries and topic limits

---

### 3.3 No Graphic or Explicit Content

To protect the community:

* No graphic descriptions of trauma, abuse, violence, or self-harm
* No step-by-step descriptions of harmful behavior
* Focus on emotions, not explicit events

Moderators may redirect or remove content that crosses this boundary.

---

### 3.4 No Romantic, Sexual, or Exclusive Dynamics

Unsaid is **not** a platform for forming romantic or exclusive relationships.

Prohibited behaviors include:

* Flirting or sexual language
* “Only I understand you” messaging
* Encouraging emotional dependence
* Requests to move conversations off-platform

---

### 3.5 No Coercion, Manipulation, or Grooming

The following are strictly prohibited:

* Pressuring users to stay online or continue conversations
* Guilt-based language (“You’re the only one who listens to me”)
* Encouraging isolation from friends, family, or professionals

---

## 4. Anonymity and Privacy

* Users must not request or share personal identifying information
* Real names, addresses, phone numbers, social media handles are not allowed
* Impersonation or false claims of professional credentials are prohibited

Unsaid prioritizes **data minimization** and complies with applicable Indian data protection laws.

---

## 5. Self-Harm and Crisis Policy

### 5.1 Allowed

* Sharing feelings of distress, overwhelm, or hopelessness
* Expressing emotions without intent or instruction

### 5.2 Not Allowed

* Encouraging self-harm or suicide
* Providing methods or instructions
* Romanticizing harm or death

### 5.3 Escalation

If a user expresses imminent risk:

* The conversation may be interrupted
* Crisis resources relevant to India will be displayed
* Moderators may take protective action

**Unsaid does not intervene or provide emergency services.**

---

## 6. Professional Support Layer

* Only verified professionals may represent themselves as therapists or counselors
* Professional interactions are time-bound and structured
* No private, ongoing dependency-forming relationships are permitted

---

## 7. Moderation & Enforcement

Unsaid uses a combination of:

* Automated safety systems
* Human moderation
* Community reporting

### Enforcement actions may include:

* Content removal
* Temporary restrictions
* Permanent suspension

Severity and intent are considered in all decisions.

---

## 8. Reporting & Support

Users are encouraged to report:

* Boundary violations
* Harassment or coercion
* Unsafe or triggering content

Reports are reviewed confidentially and without retaliation.

---

## 9. Community Responsibility

By using Unsaid, you agree to:

* Take responsibility for your own wellbeing
* Use the platform as support, not substitution
* Respect that others are here to share, not to be fixed

---

## 10. Final Note

Unsaid is built on the belief that **being heard matters** — but so do boundaries.

We are committed to continuously improving safety, inclusivity, and trust within the community.
