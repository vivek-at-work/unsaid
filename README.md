# Unsaid

**Unsaid** is an anonymous, trauma-informed support platform designed for people who want to share their thoughts and emotions without judgment, labels, or exposure of identity.

It provides a moderated, text-only community space combined with structured peer conversations and access to verified mental health professionals‚Äîwhile explicitly preventing emotional dependency and unsafe dynamics.

---

## Overview

In many regions, especially India, individuals avoid sharing emotional struggles due to stigma, fear of judgment, or lack of privacy. Unsaid addresses this gap by offering a calm, anonymous, and safety-first environment for expression and support.

Unsaid is **not a crisis service** and does not replace professional medical or psychological care.

---

## Key Principles

* **Anonymity by default**
* **Judgment-free sharing**
* **Trauma-informed design**
* **Boundaries over dependency**
* **Privacy and safety first**

---

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. Anonymous Onboarding

* No real names or photos
* System-generated aliases
* Boundary and topic preferences
* Clear safety and usage disclaimers

### 2. Community Sharing Spaces

* Topic-based, moderated text rooms
* No graphic or explicit trauma content
* Supportive reactions only (no likes or popularity metrics)

### 3. Structured Peer Conversations

* Time-bound, session-based chats
* Auto-closure to prevent over-attachment
* Post-session grounding prompts

### 4. Professional Support (Hybrid Model)

* Verified mental health professionals
* Anonymous, group-based sessions
* No private dependency-forming relationships

### 5. Safety & Moderation

* AI-assisted + human moderation
* Content flagging and reporting
* Clear enforcement and escalation flows

### 6. India-Specific Crisis Redirection

* Displays local helplines when high-risk signals are detected
* Encourages off-platform real-world support
